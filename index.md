# Welcome To HighQ Command List


## Music Commands
 
## - Play / P
  
  
 Play Music With TItle Music Or Youtube Link
 
 Example :
 
 -p Toxic BoyWithUke\
 -p https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xMHJGd3wwZk
 
## - Clear-Queue / CQ
 
 Clear All Music In The Queue
 
## - Filter
 
 Add Filter To Music
 
 Example : 
 
 -filter 8D
